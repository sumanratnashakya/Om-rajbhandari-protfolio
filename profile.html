Dr. Om Rajbhandary has been leading the overall real estate sector of Nepal for more than three decades. He is an Executive Chairman of the Brihat Group and has been responsible for developing various housing projects, providing real estate knowledge in academic institutions, running training programs and conducting research on real estate and hospitality. Being an enthusiastic entrepreneur, he has been passionately mentoring startups and helping their business grow into new level.
